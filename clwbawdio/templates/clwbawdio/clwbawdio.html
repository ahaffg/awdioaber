Clwb Awdio




Are you new to the hobby and don't know where to begin? Or do you want to try out all the latest gear but just don't have the budget? If so, why not give Clwb Awdio a try?

For a low monthly subscription, you will recieve either headphones, a DAP or an amp to try for a month. We arrange the shipping and collection for free, using industry standard damage proof packaging, so all you have to do is sit back and enjoy playing with the gear!

Also if you decide that you really like your product, you can buy it at a 10% reduction - as well as that months subscription off, of course!

What's even better is that if you take out a subscription, your first month is absolutely free! So what are you waiting for?

Our previous subscriptions or 'Clwbs' that Clwb Awdio members have recieved.

June 2023

HarmonicDyne Athena

The HarmonicDyne has evolved with the Athena. A semi-closed design that packs a lot of value, the solid build and easily likable tuning give it an interesting proposition. It has a smooth timbre, balanced vocal presence and is easy to drive.

May 2023

FiiO Q11

FiiO has focused on improving the sound quality this time around since there seems to be some features on previous models now removed. So, you lose a bass boost feature plus a line input which is a type of connectivity that’s losing popularity nowadays but, in its place, you get an upgraded and refined tuning.

April 2023

SMSL SU-1

As a sub-£100 dedicated DAC, the SMSL SU-1 is hard to beat. Considering its input flexibility, clean implementation of the AKAK4493S dac, and good dynamics, this could be a perfect upgrade or starter DAC for audiophiles assembling their first desktop audio stack. Good dynamics, small footprint and native MQA unfolding.
